After graduating from ACM, Academy of Contemporary Music, in 2014, winning the "outstanding performance student of the year" award, Tommaso has been very active in the musical theatre world as pit musician. Since 2015, he's appeared in BatBoy at Southwark Playhouse, American Idiot (Arts Theatre London, UK Tour), Joseph (UK Tour), Jersey Boys (International Tour), CATS (International Tour) and 9 to 5 (Savoy Theatre London, UK Tour). He's also been active as studio guitarist, working in partnership with Istituto Barlumen in Italy and writing guitar parts for radio dramas, TV adverts, jingles, albums and everything in between. As a transcriber, he's been active since 2014 for both personal projects and on commission. He's been active as guitar teacher since 2014.
